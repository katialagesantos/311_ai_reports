Spearman correlation between daily request counts per service type and daily weather values. Red = more requests on days with higher values of that variable. Blue = fewer requests. Sorted by highest absolute correlation.

Each row shows what share of that service's annual requests fall in each month. Services are sorted by their peak month — revealing clear seasonal clusters.
